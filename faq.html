Selling on TrustSell

To post an ad: 1) Click the "Sell" button in the bottom navigation, 2) Select the appropriate category, 3) Fill in the details and upload photos, 4) Set your price and location, 5) Review and publish your ad.

A good ad includes: Clear, well-lit photos from multiple angles, an honest and detailed description, accurate pricing, proper category selection, and quick response to buyer inquiries.

Ads typically remain active for 30 days. You can renew your ad before it expires to keep it visible. Sold items should be marked as sold to maintain platform accuracy.

Yes, you can edit your ads at any time through the "My Ads" section. Changes may take a few minutes to appear across the platform.

Buying on TrustSell

Click the "Safe Chat" button on any listing to start a conversation with the seller. All communications should stay within the TrustSell platform for your safety.

Always prioritize safety: Meet in public places, bring a friend, inspect items thoroughly, and trust your instincts. Avoid carrying large amounts of cash and never share personal financial information.

Use the search bar at the top of any page, then refine your results using filters for price range, location, condition, and other attributes. You can also browse by category for broader searches.
